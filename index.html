Meet Dr. Shivani (BPT, MPT Ortho)

Healing begins with understanding the body. Dr. Shivani is a dedicated orthopedics and sports physiotherapist with over a decade of clinical experience in helping patients recover from severe musculoskeletal conditions, orthopedic discomforts, and post-surgery limitations.
